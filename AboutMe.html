Something about me...

I am a daughter, sister, wife, mother, friend ... born and raised in my beautiful Macedonia (Europe).
One decade ago life took us to USA Chicago ( The land of the free, and the land of the brave), Here with my husband we started to build our new life from scratch.
After eight years of being a stay-at-home mom for my two boys (whom I love endlessly, of course), I decided it was time to return to my professional life. I wanted to work on something that would fulfill both me and my dreams.
After a long research, the missing puzzle I discovered was in the UX/UI field. Art and design in various forms and fields have always been my passion and an integral part of my life.
I became immediately captivated by the world of UX/UI and its boundless creativity in various fields. What I love most is UX research, as I wholeheartedly believe that everything has its roots and can flourish with daily care and love.
