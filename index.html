My UX Skills

User Research

A well-structured research plan serves as the foundation of any project, and, for me, it is paramount to start with a solid plan. I begin by defining objectives and formulating relevant questions. Conducting one-on-one interviews and asking a multitude of 'why' questions are integral components of my approach, as they allow me to gain deeper insights into user needs and behaviors.

User Interface Design

After crafting a well-structured UX research plan and conducting thorough analysis, I derive immense satisfaction from crafting designs that users will genuinely adore and engage with when using the product. My process often starts with low-fidelity sketches, where I pour all my ideas onto paper. I then conduct testing to gauge user acceptance and pinpoint areas that require further refinement.

Web Development

For me, web development represents both an art and a science. It involves continuous adaptation and evolution. However, the most rewarding moment comes when I witness a project taking shape, where all my creative ideas and designs come to life. Building a website, where each element seamlessly integrates with the CSS styles, feels like the perfect blend of science and art, akin to performing magic.
